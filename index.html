About Sharon

With over 30 years of experience as a Master Colourist, Sharon brings salon-quality styling and colour to the comfort of your home. Specialising in women’s hair styling and men’s barbering, Hair by Sharon is a unique mobile salon offering elegance and convenience.

The Mobile Salon Experience

Hair by Sharon isn’t just about hair — it’s about convenience and comfort. With my fully-equipped mobile salon van, you’ll enjoy a professional salon experience without leaving your home.

  • ✔ Fully equipped with salon chair, mirrors & professional tools
  • ✔ No travel or parking stress — I come to you
  • ✔ Perfect for busy professionals, parents, and those who prefer comfort at home
